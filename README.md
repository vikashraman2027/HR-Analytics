# HR Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
The **HR Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, departmental performance, and employee satisfaction metrics.

This dashboard helps HR teams and management identify attrition patterns, understand workforce distribution, and make data-driven decisions to improve employee retention and organizational performance.

---

# Objectives
- Analyze employee attrition trends
- Identify departments with high attrition
- Understand employee demographics and experience levels
- Evaluate job satisfaction across job roles
- Monitor workforce distribution and salary impact

---

# Tools & Technologies Used
- Power BI
- Excel / CSV Dataset
- Data Cleaning & Transformation
- Data Visualization
- HR Analytics & Reporting

---

# Key KPI Metrics
The dashboard provides high-level HR insights including:

- Total Employees: **1,403**
- Active Employees: **1,176**
- Attrition Count: **227**
- Attrition Rate: **16.18%**
- Average Employee Age: **36.97**
- Average Experience: **7.05 Years**

# Key Business Insights
- Administration and Sales departments show higher attrition rates.
- Employees with lower salary slabs experience higher attrition.
- Most employees fall within the 26–35 age group.
- Attrition is comparatively higher among male employees.
- Employees with lower experience levels are more likely to leave the organization.

---

# Business Recommendations
- Improve employee engagement programs in high-attrition departments.
- Re-evaluate compensation strategies for lower salary slabs.
- Enhance career growth opportunities for early-stage employees.
- Conduct satisfaction surveys to identify workplace concerns.
- Implement targeted retention strategies for critical job roles.
